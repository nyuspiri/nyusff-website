Recognizing exceptional films that demonstrate outstanding storytelling, technical achievement, and innovative perspectives in sports filmmaking.

Honorable Mentions Showcase

Recognition of Excellence

Our honorable mentions recognize films that stood out during the jury review process for their exceptional qualities, unique perspectives, or innovative approaches to sports storytelling.

What Earns an Honorable Mention

  • Outstanding cinematography or technical achievement
  • Innovative storytelling techniques
  • Unique cultural perspectives on sports
  • Compelling character development
  • Creative use of sports as a narrative device
  • Strong emotional impact and audience engagement

Past Recognition

In 2024, we recognized 5 honorable mentions alongside our 19 official selections, demonstrating the high caliber of submissions we receive from filmmakers worldwide.
