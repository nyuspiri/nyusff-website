Submit Your Film

Share your sports story with the world

Submission Guidelines

The NYU Sports Film Festival welcomes submissions from filmmakers around the world who tell compelling sports stories. We celebrate the diverse ways athletics intersect with human experience.

Eligible Categories

  • Short Films: Fiction, documentary, and experimental films up to 40 minutes
  • Student Films: Films created by current students or recent graduates
  • Music Videos: Sports-themed music videos
  • Commercial Work: Advertising and branded content related to sports

Requirements

  • Films must have a clear connection to sports, athletics, or physical competition
  • Maximum runtime of 40 minutes for narrative and documentary films
  • Films must be completed within the last 3 years
  • All submissions must include English subtitles if not in English

How to Submit

Submissions are accepted through FilmFreeway and other major festival platforms. Early bird deadlines offer reduced submission fees.

Awards

  • Best Short Film
  • Best Documentary
  • Best Student Film
  • Audience Choice Award
  • NYU Production Lab Award

Questions?

Contact us at nyusportsfilmfestival@nyu.edu for submission inquiries.
