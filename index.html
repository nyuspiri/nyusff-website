THE NYU Sports Film Festival

Co-Produced by the NYU Production Lab and NYU Athletics

Announcements

JOIN US AT THE 5TH ANNUAL NYU SPORTS FILM FESTIVAL!

Friday, April 25th–Saturday, April 26th

View the Detailed Schedule and Register to Attend: FREE RSVP HERE

Celebrate sports media with us at the Angelika Film Center, where a curated selection of short films from around the world will be screened in competition for festival awards! Our events outside of the screenings will include a panel on the Diverse Mediums of Sports Storytelling and a Pitch Contest where you can get a chance to pitch your sports film ideas in front of people that can turn them into reality! The 2025 festival produced by the NYU Production Lab, NYU Athletics, and the NYU Tisch Institute for Global Sport will feature intimate sessions with award-winning sports media veterans who will share invaluable insights!

"From Raging Bull to He Got Game to The Rider, NYU filmmakers have brought powerful sports stories to the screen. The NYU Sports Film Festival celebrates that great tradition."

John Tintori, director, screenwriter, and Oscar-winning film editor
