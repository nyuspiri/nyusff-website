Partner with the NYU Sports Film Festival to support emerging voices in sports storytelling while reaching a dynamic audience of filmmakers, athletes, and media professionals.

Why Partner with NYUSFF?

The NYU Sports Film Festival brings together the worlds of athletics and cinema, creating unique opportunities for brands to connect with passionate audiences who value excellence, creativity, and authentic storytelling.

Targeted Audience

Reach filmmakers, sports professionals, NYU community members, and media industry leaders in one focused event.

Brand Visibility

Showcase your brand through event signage, digital platforms, and promotional materials across our festival weekend.

Network Access

Connect with emerging talent, industry professionals, and thought leaders in sports media and filmmaking.

Content Association

Align your brand with compelling stories that celebrate the human spirit and athletic achievement.
