About the Festival

Celebrating Sports Media Excellence

Festival History

From Raging Bull to He Got Game to The Rider, NYU filmmakers have brought powerful sports stories to the screen. The NYU Sports Film Festival celebrates that great tradition, now in its 5th year of honoring excellence in sports media.

The festival has grown from a small gathering of sports enthusiasts and filmmakers to a prestigious event that attracts submissions from around the world. Each year, we showcase the best in sports storytelling, from intimate documentaries to high-energy action pieces.

What We Do

  • Screen curated short films from around the world in competition for festival awards
  • Host panels on diverse mediums of sports storytelling
  • Conduct pitch contests where filmmakers can present ideas to industry professionals
  • Feature intimate sessions with award-winning sports media veterans

Our Partners

Co-produced by the NYU Production Lab, NYU Athletics, and the NYU Tisch Institute for Global Sport. We're proud to work with these organizations to bring together the worlds of academics, athletics, and media.
