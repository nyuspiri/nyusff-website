6th Annual NYUSFF

The 2026 NYU Sports Film Festival is currently in development. Building on the success of our previous five years, we're planning our most ambitious festival yet.

2026 Festival Vision

What to Expect

The 6th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival will continue our tradition of celebrating the powerful intersection of sports and cinema, featuring:

  • International film submissions from emerging and established filmmakers
  • Prestigious venue screenings in New York City
  • Industry panels with sports media professionals
  • Networking opportunities for filmmakers and industry professionals
  • Student workshops and educational programming
  • Awards ceremony celebrating excellence in sports storytelling

Festival Timeline

Specific dates and submission deadlines for 2026 will be announced soon. Stay tuned for updates on when you can submit your films and RSVP for events.
