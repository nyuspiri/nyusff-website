2026 Official Selections

The official selections for the 6th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival will be announced following our submission period and jury review process.

2026 Film Showcase

Selection Process

Our jury of industry professionals, including representatives from major media companies, carefully reviews all submissions to select the most compelling and innovative sports films from around the world.

What We Look For

  • Compelling storytelling that captures the human spirit in sports
  • Technical excellence in filmmaking
  • Unique perspectives on athletic experiences
  • Cultural diversity and global reach
  • Innovation in sports documentary and narrative filmmaking

Previous Years

In 2025, we selected 12 official films from submissions representing 22 U.S. states and 17 nations. Our festival continues to grow in international recognition and participation each year.
