5th Annual NYUSFF TV Special Now Airing on SNY!

The 5th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival television special is now broadcasting on SportsNet New York (SNY), featuring highlights from our 2025 festival, award-winning films, and exclusive filmmaker interviews.

Watch the 5th Annual Special on SNY

We're excited to announce that the 5th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival television special is now airing on SportsNet New York (SNY). This hour-long program showcases the best moments from our 2025 festival, including exclusive interviews with filmmakers, highlights from our award-winning films, and behind-the-scenes footage from the festival events.

The special features our award-winning films including Kingdome, The Eight Count, Marie's Ambition, and more, giving viewers an intimate look at the powerful stories that define sports cinema today.

Broadcasting Schedule

The 5th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival Special will air multiple times throughout the month. Check your local SNY listings or visit SNY.tv for the complete broadcast schedule and streaming options.

What to Expect

  • Exclusive filmmaker interviews and behind-the-scenes content
  • Highlights from award-winning films and honorable mentions
  • Coverage of our Angelika Film Center screenings
  • Insights from industry professionals and festival guests
  • A look at the future of sports filmmaking

About SNY Partnership

SportsNet New York has been a key partner of the NYU Sports Film Festival since our inception, helping us reach broader audiences and celebrate the intersection of sports and cinema. This annual television special has become a tradition that brings our festival experience to viewers across the tri-state area and beyond.

The partnership with SNY allows us to extend the impact of these powerful stories beyond our festival walls, reaching sports fans and film enthusiasts who share our passion for authentic storytelling.

Don't miss this opportunity to experience the 5th Annual NYU Sports Film Festival from the comfort of your home. Tune in to SNY and discover the stories that are shaping the future of sports cinema.
